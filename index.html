How does this work?

Overcollaterized token

When Stablecoins are minted, users are providing 50% more collateral, so crypto price fluctations won't affect your money!

Managed by community

Stablecoin is ran by its vast community, and the smart contracts are paused on extreme market conditions or use embergency funds

Value transfers

By transferring Stablecoins, users also transfer that part of the collateral so anyone can swap their USD back to let's say Ethereum (even if they haven't minted their tokens originally)

Value stability

Stablecoin is pegged to the USA Dollar in a 1:1 ratio. Stablecoin is a great way to hold your high volatility crypto earnings in a more stable form. You can even shop with your stablecoins!
